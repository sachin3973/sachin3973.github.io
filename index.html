Class 12th Business Studies Chapter- Marketing- Marketing Mix

In this lecture we are covering three topics:
Marketing mix
Factors affecting market decisions
Elements of marketing mix

Marketing mix

The marketing mix deals with the way in which a business uses prices, product, distribution and promotion to market and sell its product. It is known as "mix" because each ingredient affects the other and mix must overall be suitable to the target customer.

Factors affecting market decisions

These can be broadly divided into two categories:
1. Controable Factors
2. Non Controable Factors

Elements of marketing mix

The marketing mix is a tool that is made up of four unique but interconnected and interdependent variables. These are called the 4P's and are product, price, promotion, and place. These four components help determine a clear and effective strategy to bring a product to market
